The Four Forces Shaping 2025

These interconnected trends define the modern social media experience and create a powerful feedback loop.

🎬

1. The Pivot to Video

Video is the default language of social media. Short-form content drives discovery, while a resurgence in long-form caters to a demand for deeper storytelling.

🛒

2. Social as Search & Storefront

Younger generations increasingly use platforms like TikTok for search, while integrated shopping features have dissolved the barrier between content and commerce.

🤖

3. The AI Revolution

From hyper-personalized "For You" feeds to creator-assistance tools and chatbot customer service, AI is a visible, transformative force shaping the entire experience.

❤️

4. The Quest for Authenticity

In response to polished feeds, users are drawn to raw, unfiltered content and niche communities, demanding a more genuine connection with brands and creators.

➡️
🔁

The Feedback Loop

AI enhances video discovery, which powers social commerce. This saturation drives users to seek authentic communities, creating a cycle that constantly redefines strategy.
